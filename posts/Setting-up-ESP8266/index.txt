11:T522,<h1 id="esp8266-diagram">esp8266 Diagram</h1>
<p><img src="/images/8a1901b6b834b9f702bc8b98f1865959.png" alt=""></p>
<ol>
<li><p>In your Arduino IDE, go to File&gt; Preferences<br><img src="/images/25c1dcb22468c322c64fae03eeb38268.png" alt=""></p>
</li>
<li><p>Enter the following into the “Additional Board Manager URLs” field:</p>
</li>
</ol>
<pre><code> http://arduino.esp8266.com/stable/package_esp8266com_index.json
</code></pre>
<p>click the “OK” button:<br>	<img src="/images/cb2a6a5bc38d8fb0ed3c738c7666fb57.png" alt=""><br><strong>Note</strong>:if you already have the ESP8266 boards URL, you can separate the URLs with a comma as follows</p>
<pre><code class="language-txt">https://dl.espressif.com/dl/package_esp32_index.json, http://arduino.esp8266.com/stable/package_esp8266com_index.json
</code></pre>
<ol start="3">
<li><p>Open the Boards Manager. Go to Tools &gt; Board &gt; Boards Manager…<br> <img src="/images/dca58c790d2582c1a35485c0f4eeb457.png" alt=""></p>
</li>
<li><p>Search for ESP8266 and press install button for the “ESP32 by Espressif Systems“:<br> <img src="/images/4cf3bafdc181e501c758294e42974b24.png" alt=""></p>
</li>
<li><p>That’s it. It should be installed after a few seconds.<br> <img src="/images/aa1a8bef9d8e750951f1346f42f92aed.png" alt=""></p>
</li>
</ol>
